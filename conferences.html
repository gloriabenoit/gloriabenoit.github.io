Current affiliation

I currently am a research engineer at Institut Pasteur, in Paris, within the Statistical Genetics team led by Hugues Aschard. This follows my 6 month internship in the same team under supervision by Léo Henches, a permanent research engineer.

Latest appearances

During my intership at Institut Pasteur, I was lucky to attend two conferences: the International symposium on Human Genomics (ISHG) in Paris, France, in May 2025, and the European Mathematical Genetics Meeting (EMGM) in Brest, France, in April 2025. During these two meetings, my supervisor Léo Henches and I had the opportunity to present our work on using DNAnexus to perform a GWAS study on UK Biobank data through a poster presentation.
