Current affiliation

I currently am a research engineer at Institut Pasteur, in Paris, within the Statistical Genetics team led by Hugues Aschard. This follows my 6 month internship in the same team under supervision by Léo Henches, a permanent research engineer.

Latest appearances

During my intership at Institut Pasteur, I was lucky to attend two conferences: the International symposium on Human Genomics (ISHG) in Paris, France, in May 2025, and the European Mathematical Genetics Meeting (EMGM) in Brest, France, in April 2025. During these two meetings, my supervisor Léo Henches and I had the opportunity to present our work on using DNAnexus to perform a GWAS study on UK Biobank data through a poster presentation.

Experience and Education

2025 - Now

Research Engineer (8 month)

Institut Pasteur

In collaboration with Michael Cho's team at the Brigham and Women's Hospital in Boston, as part of the NIH project 1R01HL168199-01A1, I have studied the heterogeneity of chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD).

I have created a single pipeline to detect pleiotropic patterns across using six approaches: SUPERGNOVA and HDL-L,which estimate local genetic correlations; and GFA, Guide, GLEANR and FactorGo which derive latent factors.

2024 - 2025

Master 2 in Bioinformatics

Université Paris Cité

Internship: Efficient cloud computing in large scale genetic data (Jan-Jun 2025, Pasteur Institute, Léo Henches)

The aim of the internship is to transition from using UK Biobank data locally to their DNAnexus platform. The goal is to produce a range of tools that will make it easy and straightforward to perform a GWAS on DNAnexus.

2023 - 2024

Master 1 in Bioinformatics

Université Paris Cité

Internship: Experimental data processing in the mass spectrometric analysis of intact proteins (Mar-Jun 2024, Jacques Monod Institute, Jean-Michel Camadro)

The aim of the internship is to produce software suitable for processing the signal obtained by mass spectrometry for intact proteins. The development is carried out in Python, and takes into account all data processing, as well as data analysis.
